Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pakage (~2 cups) chocolate chips

Currently multipling by: 1

Directions

    Preheat oven to 375* F. Mix both sugars, butter, and egg in a large
bowl. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in chocolate chips. Bake 
until light brown, about 8-10 minutes. Let sit on pan for a couple of minutes,
then transfer to a rack until cool.

    Makes about 24 cookies.
