Tuna Biscuts

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • shredded cheese
  • tuna

Directions

Mix dry ingredients. Add wet ingredients. Roll into rectangles. Spread
shredded cheese and tuna. Roll up. Cut up. Cook at 400* F for 15 minutes.
